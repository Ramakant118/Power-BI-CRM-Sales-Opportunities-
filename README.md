**B2B Sales Pipeline Dashboard (Power BI)**

**Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive B2B Sales Pipeline Dashboard built using Power BI. The dataset represents a fictitious company that sells computer hardware and contains information about accounts, products, sales teams, opportunities, and deal stages.

The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, opportunity distribution, product profitability, and sales team effectiveness. It enables stakeholders to quickly evaluate pipeline health and identify areas where performance can be improved.

**Business Objective**

The goal of this analysis is to evaluate the performance of the sales pipeline and answer key business questions such as:

1 Which sales teams are performing better compared to others?
2 Are any sales agents underperforming?
3 Are there quarter-over-quarter trends in sales opportunities?
4 Which products generate the highest win rates?
5 Which products and regions contribute the most revenue and profit?

Dashboard Features

**1. Sales Pipeline Overview**

The first dashboard provides a high-level overview of the sales pipeline performance.

Key metrics include:

Won Deals: Total number of successfully closed deals
Lost Deals: Total number of deals that were not converted
Win Rate: Ratio of won deals to total opportunities
Total Opportunities: Number of potential deals in the pipeline
Average Deal Size: Average value of sales opportunities

Visual insights include:

Product performance based on deal counts
Distribution of deals across pipeline stages (Won, Lost, Engaging, Prospecting)
Quarterly trends of deal counts across products
Sales agent performance comparison

**2. Revenue and Product Performance Dashboard**

The second dashboard focuses on revenue generation and product success.

Key insights include:

Total revenue generated by different products and pricing tiers
Opportunity distribution based on product and price
Comparison of revenue generated by each sales agent
Win rate comparison across products
Interactive filters for sector and product selection

Key Insights

**Sales Team Performance**

The dashboard helps identify top-performing sales agents and compare their deal closures and revenue contributions.

**Underperforming Sales Agents**

Agents with lower deal closures or revenue contribution can be identified for further training or strategy adjustments.

**Quarter-over-Quarter Trends**

Quarterly trends highlight changes in sales activity, helping businesses understand seasonal patterns and pipeline growth.

**Product Win Rate Analysis**

Products with higher win rates indicate stronger market demand and better sales performance.

**Tools & Technologies**

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
Data Modeling – Relationships between sales agents, products, and opportunities
DAX Measures – Calculations such as win rate, revenue metrics, and deal counts
